Suicidal Behaviour


The trades of suicidal bhevaiour cannot be genralised, it's calcualted that the chances of suicidal instinct is higher in individuals suffering from a range of disorders. Statistically calculated that the chnaces of suicide for a victim suffering from bipolor disorder, severe depression, and PTSD is extremely high, it is quite often noticed that due to the mental health stigam, many victims suffering these conditions often undermine their symptoms which leads to severe downfalls in their condition.

Various other disorders may also leave suicidal behaviour in the victim, the symptoms of such behaviour isn't very easily validated but please help the ones around you, because for all of us, it's okay to not be okay

The few common symptoms most identified are mentioned below, please understand that every individual's symptoms differ from the other. Under any serious circumstances, we strongly urge you to take medical help.

  • Depression
  • Self-destructive behaviour
  • Hopelessness
  • Withdrawal from any activity
  • Talking about suicide
  • Exessive sleeping or no sleeping at all


Anxiety Disorder


When panic and anxiety symptoms gradually lead into anxiety attacks and panic attacks, the chances of the person suffering from anxiety disorder increases. Anxiety has become quite a common feeling among individuals, it’s your brain’s way of dealing with stress and warning you of potential danger ahead, this defines to be occasional anxiety. it's important to differentiate between occasional anxeity and an anxiety disorder.

The few common symptoms most identified are mentioned below, please understand that every individual's symptoms differ from the other. Under any serious circumstances, we strongly urge you to take medical help.

  • Deep fealings of fear, panic and uneasiness.
  • Hyperventilation: breathing heavingly and rapidly with complete uneasiness.
  • Sweating out of panic in a room temperature place.
  • Nauseausness
  • Tachycardia: heartrate over 100 beats per minute
  • Rumination: Repeatedly stressing over a particular topic


Personality Disorder


An individual suffering from personality disorder often behaves very differently compared to an average person. This disorder is known to be a mental illness that unfortunately segregate the victim from the genral public. The different types of personality disorders are sectioned into three clusters by taking the the symptoms and the characteristics under consideration.

The few common symptoms most identified are mentioned below, please understand that every individual's symptoms differ from the other. Under any serious circumstances, we strongly urge you to take medical help.

  • Cluster A is characterised as unconventional, strange or odd thinking which affects the behaviour of the individual simultaneously. They consist of paranoid personality disorder, schizoid personality disorder, schizotypal personality disorder.
  • Cluster B is characterised as overdramtic, introverted and unpredictable thinking which affects the behaviour of the individual simultaneously. They consist of antisocial personality disorder, borderline personality disorder, histrionic personality disorder and narcissistic personality disorder.
  • Cluster C is characterised as frightened, anxious and panicked thinking which affects the behaviour of the individual simultaneously. They consist of avoidant personality disorder, dependent personality disorder and obsessive-compulsive personality disorder.


Obsessive Compulsive Disorder (OCD)


Obsessions are the thoughts that the mind of the victim wants to compulse out, the thoughts usually consist of distressing, uneasy and intense feeling due to unwanted and intrusive impression, they constantly appear in the mind of the victim affecting their dialy activities.

It is important to realise that OCD is far different from the common obsessions or compulsions that an average person experiences, compulsions are the urgent thought to immediatly remove the obbsessive feeling that has been troubling the mind of the victim.

It is often noticed that the victim are unaware of their disorder which leaves them to think that it is normal to have such intense feelings, this usually triggers extreme anxiety issues which may lead to panic attacks.

Mentioned above are just a few common behaviour identified for this disorder, please understand that every individual's symptoms differ from the other. Under any serious circumstances, we strongly urge you to take medical help.



Sleeping Disorder


Sleeping disorder is caused due to a group of reasons which affects the regular sleeping schedule of an individual. The number of people suffering from sleeping disorders are increasing consecutively. Some days, we experience a rough day where we are not able to have our beauty sleep due to stress or perhaps health issues or hectic schedules, the long term case of irregualr sleep where it starts to interfer the daily life, it may be a sign of sleeping disorder.

There are quite a few types of sleeping disorders, the most common sleeping disorder statistically has been proven to be Insomnia, which is defned as unable to fall asleep at all or remain asleep. Every victim experience this disorder for multiple reasons, such as anxiety, jet lag, PTSD etc.

The few common symptoms most identified are mentioned below, please understand that every individual's symptoms differ from the other. Under any serious circumstances, we strongly urge you to take medical help.

  • Difficulty falling asleep
  • Anxiety
  • Stress disorder
  • It is commonly noticed that saleeping disorder occurs due to victim's other health conditions, in such cases, it's highly recommended to take immediate medical help.


Substance Abuse Disorder


This disorder is caused due to drug addications, which affects the victim's behaviour and health severely. In most countries, the consumption of certain drugs with no medical purpose or prescription is considered illegal by the law. The addiction of the victim grows out to incline them to illegal ways to consume the known drug, many of them are receiving treatment in rehabilitation centers to control the same.

Different drugs have different dangers attached to it. Statistically reproted, if the victims suffering from this disorder are not able to consume their habital high dose, it will most definitely effect their health condition.

The few common symptoms most identified are mentioned below, please understand that every individual's symptoms differ from the other. Under any serious circumstances, we strongly urge you to take medical help.

  • The constant consumption of the drug, where it becomes a habit of daily rotuine.
  • Strong thoughts of consuming drugs where it starts to affect the regular routine of the victim.
  • Expriencing withdrawal health conditions when you try to get rid of the drug abuse.
  • Out of control of your actions which may lead you to illegal drug consumption.


Post Traumatic Stress Disorder


Triggered due to a horrifying incident that the victim might have witnessed or experienced, which leaves them in constant fear, stress, uneasiness or even repeatded flashback or nightmares related to the incident which in due time effects their daily routine. The symptoms of PTSD are very oftenly taken lightly in the inital period which may turn out to be quite crucial as the delay may lead to other medical disorders for the victim.

The few common symptoms most identified are mentioned below, please understand that every individual's symptoms differ from the other. Under any serious circumstances, we strongly urge you to take medical help.

  • Repeated flashbacks and visuals of the incident leading to extreme anxiousness and fear.
  • Sensation of nauseousness, pain, sweat, trembling, headaches etc.
  • Sudden extreme distress due to remeberance of the incident or at times, just naturally.
  • Trust issues, fear to trust even their loving ones.
  • Self-destructive behaviour.
  • Many symptoms of PTSD could also be the leading symptoms of other disorders, it's highly recommened to take immediate medical help to get your diagnosis.
