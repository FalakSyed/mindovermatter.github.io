All you need to know

Why should we navigate mental health?

Mental health is just as important as physical health, taking care of our mental health helps avoid any serious medical health issues in the future. Just like other parts of our body, our brain needs nuturing and care.

How important is mental health stability?

A healthy mind helps to be productive and active, allows your thoughts to be calm and positive. A continous attempt of taking care of your mental health will help you avoid any future possible issues.

What is mental health stigma?

This stigma is defined as the negative and descriminatory approach towards mental health issues, this stigma exists in the public as well as the victim, where they are ashamed and feel humilated, this is known to ultimately have a worse affect on the victim's mental health.

Can we find out the cause of mental health issues?

This highly depends on the issue you are facing, it's not decent to subjectify it to a certain cause. It's best advice to get a medical checkup done to ensure you are not misleaded in the wrong path.

What does it mean to be sectioned?

This is most oftenly for the extreme cases of mental health issues, it refers to being admitted in the hospital with or without the consent of the patient, authorised by the Mental Health Act.

How do you know if you are suffering from mental illness?

This website offers a wide range of possible symptoms in the discover, please note that these are calculated symptoms of the majority, all individuals's issues differ, please take medical help for any confirmation.

How can you help someone with a mental health problem?

You should make them feel that they belong to your cluster and give them the comfort that is required. Let them open up to you at their own space. If there's a serious issue, its strongly urged to take immediate medical help.

How normal is mental health issues in 2020?

With the ongoing pandemic, majority of the individuals all over the world are battling to keep their mental health stable and trying to get rid of the monotony. It's definitely not an easy period but this shall too pass and this time, we will come out of it stronger and happier.
